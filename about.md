I am a software developer and signal processing engineer. I enjoy designing new algorithms and writing fast and simple code in C++, C and Python. My professional interests include audio and sonar processing, beamforming, detection and classification, automated signal characterisation, image processing and machine learning.

I am also interested in biomedical engineering and for few years I was contributing to research at the Department of Cardiology at Westmead Public Hospital. We worked on improving safety and efficiency of radiofrequency ablation and Electrical Impedance Tomography, a new imaging technique.

To learn more about my projects please visit the [projects](http://www.kosobrodov.net/projects/) page.
